## Quick Bodyweight Circuit
Do 2-3 rounds with 30 seconds rest between exercises.

### Round
1. **Squats** — 15 reps. Stand shoulder-width apart, sit back as if into a chair, push through heels to stand
2. **Push-ups** — 10-15 reps. Modified on knees if needed. Keep core tight, chest touches floor
3. **Lunges** — 10 each leg. Step forward, lower back knee toward floor, push back to standing
4. **Plank** — Hold 30-45 seconds. Forearms on ground, body in a straight line, engage core
5. **Jumping Jacks** — 20 reps. Get your heart rate up, breathe rhythmically

### Cool Down
- Walk in place for 30 seconds
- Touch your toes and hold 15 seconds
- Quad stretch: pull each heel to glutes, hold 15 seconds

## Tips
💡 No equipment needed — do it anywhere with a bit of floor space.
💡 Modify intensity: fewer reps or wall push-ups if starting out.
💡 Total time: ~15 minutes for 2 rounds.

## Why It Helps
Short bodyweight workouts maintain muscle strength, improve posture, boost metabolism, and release endorphins. Even 15 minutes of exercise significantly reduces the health risks of prolonged sitting.

⚠️ Warm up first. Skip if you have any pain or injury.
