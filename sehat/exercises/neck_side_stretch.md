## Steps
1. Sit tall with shoulders relaxed
2. Slowly tilt your head to the **RIGHT**, bringing your ear toward your shoulder
3. You should feel a gentle stretch on the **LEFT** side of your neck
4. Hold for 15-20 seconds
5. Return to center slowly
6. Repeat on the **LEFT** side
7. Do 3 repetitions on each side

## Tips
💡 Don't raise your shoulder to meet your ear — keep shoulders down.
💡 For a deeper stretch, gently place your hand on top of your head (don't pull).

## Why It Helps
Side stretches target the scalene muscles and upper trapezius, which shorten and tighten from prolonged computer use. Regular stretching maintains neck range of motion and reduces stiffness.

⚠️ Never force the stretch. Stop if you feel pain or tingling.
