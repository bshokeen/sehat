## Steps
1. Stop looking at your screen
2. Look at something at least 20 feet (6 meters) away
3. Focus on it for 20 seconds
4. Blink several times before returning to screen

## Tips
💡 Look out a window if possible — natural light is a bonus.
💡 Set your gaze on a tree, building, or distant object.

## Why It Helps
The 20-20-20 rule (American Academy of Ophthalmology) reduces digital eye strain by relaxing the ciliary muscles that focus your lens. Prolonged near-focus causes eye fatigue, dryness, and headaches.
