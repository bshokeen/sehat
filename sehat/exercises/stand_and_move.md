## Steps
1. Stand up from your chair
2. Walk around for 1-2 minutes (refill water, go to another room)
3. Do 10 calf raises (rise up on toes, hold 2 sec, lower)
4. Do 5 gentle hip circles in each direction
5. Shake out your arms and hands for 10 seconds
6. Return to your desk refreshed

## Tips
💡 Combine with a water refill — hydration and movement in one trip.
💡 If space allows, walk up and down stairs once.

## Why It Helps
Sitting for more than 60 minutes reduces blood flow to muscles and increases stiffness. Brief movement breaks restore circulation, reduce back/hip tension, and improve concentration. OSHA recommends standing breaks every 45-60 minutes.
