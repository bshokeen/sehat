## Steps
1. Pause what you're doing
2. Take a sip or drink a full glass of water
3. If your bottle is empty, refill it now

## Tips
💡 Keep a water bottle at your desk — visible = remembered.
💡 Room temperature water is absorbed faster than cold water.
💡 Add a slice of lemon or cucumber if plain water feels boring.
💡 Tea, coconut water, and infused water all count toward hydration.

## Why It Helps
Even mild dehydration (1-2%) impairs concentration, mood, and working memory. Office workers lose water through breathing, screen-induced reduced blinking, and air conditioning. Regular sips prevent the "suddenly parched" feeling and keep energy levels steady throughout the day. Aim for 8+ glasses across the workday.
