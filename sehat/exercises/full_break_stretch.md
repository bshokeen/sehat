## Steps
1. Stand up and walk away from your desk for at least 5 minutes
2. Do a full-body stretch: arms overhead, side bends, touch your toes
3. Roll your neck gently in both directions
4. Do 10 deep breaths — in through nose, out through mouth
5. Refill your water, grab a healthy snack if needed
6. Walk around — stairs, hallway, or outside if possible

## Tips
💡 Get outside if you can — sunlight resets your circadian rhythm and boosts alertness.
💡 Avoid checking your phone during the break — give your eyes and mind a real rest.
💡 Use this break to refill water and use the restroom.

## Why It Helps
Extended sitting compresses spinal discs, tightens hip flexors, and reduces blood flow to your legs. A proper 10-minute break every 2 hours reverses these effects and restores mental focus. Studies show productivity increases significantly with regular full breaks.
