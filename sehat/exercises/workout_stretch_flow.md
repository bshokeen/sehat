## Stretch & Mobility Flow
A gentle full-body stretch routine. Hold each position for 20-30 seconds.

### Upper Body
1. **Neck rolls** — Slow circles, 5 each direction
2. **Shoulder circles** — Large circles, 10 forward, 10 backward
3. **Chest opener** — Clasp hands behind back, lift arms, squeeze shoulder blades
4. **Tricep stretch** — Reach one arm overhead, bend elbow, gently push with other hand
5. **Wrist circles** — 10 each direction, then flex/extend each wrist

### Lower Body
1. **Standing quad stretch** — Pull heel to glutes, hold each leg 20 seconds
2. **Hamstring stretch** — Place heel on a low surface, lean forward gently
3. **Hip circles** — Hands on hips, large circles, 10 each direction
4. **Calf raises** — 15 slow reps, pause at the top for 2 seconds
5. **Ankle circles** — 10 each direction, each foot

### Core & Back
1. **Cat-cow** — On all fours, arch and round your back, 10 reps
2. **Child's pose** — Kneel, reach arms forward on the floor, hold 30 seconds
3. **Seated twist** — Sit cross-legged, twist gently each direction, hold 15 seconds

## Tips
💡 Breathe deeply into each stretch — exhale to go deeper.
💡 Never bounce or force a stretch. Gentle tension, not pain.
💡 Great as a morning wake-up or evening wind-down routine.

## Why It Helps
Regular stretching improves flexibility, reduces muscle tension, enhances circulation, and prevents injury. A 10-minute flow is enough to counteract hours of sitting and improve overall mobility.
