## Steps

### Shrugs (10 reps)
1. Sit or stand tall with arms at your sides
2. Raise both shoulders up toward your ears
3. Hold for 3 seconds
4. Release and let shoulders drop
5. Repeat 10 times

### Rolls (5 each direction)
1. Roll both shoulders forward in a circular motion — 5 times
2. Roll both shoulders backward in a circular motion — 5 times

## Tips
💡 Breathe out as you release the shrug — let the tension go.
💡 Keep your neck relaxed during rolls.

## Why It Helps
Shoulder tension from typing/mousing transfers directly to neck muscles. Shrugs and rolls release the upper trapezius — the #1 tension holder for desk workers.
